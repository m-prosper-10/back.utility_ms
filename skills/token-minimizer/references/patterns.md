# Token Compression Patterns

## Plans

- Use 2-4 steps.
- Omit steps that are obvious from the next command.

## Reviews

- Lead with findings.
- For no findings, say that directly and mention residual risk in one sentence.

## Change Summaries

- Prefer `Changed X to do Y` over file-by-file narration.
- Mention verification in one clause: `Tests passed` or `Not verified`.
